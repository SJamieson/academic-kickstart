# Bio

I am a PhD student in the [MIT-WHOI Joint Program](http://mit.whoi.edu/) studying Applied Ocean Science and Engineering. I'm pursuing the development of federated multi-robot systems that can autonomously explore unfamiliar environments where communication is limited, such as the deep sea. My research focus is on _value of information_ computation, through which each robot can determine which information queries and world observations would best support the team's success.

I am co-supervised by [Dr. Yogesh Girdhar](https://www.whoi.edu/profile/ygirdhar) (<a href="https://www.whoi.edu" target="_blank" rel="noopener">WHOI</a>) and [Prof. Jonathan P. How](http://www.mit.edu/people/jhow/) (<a href="https://www.mit.edu" target="_blank" rel="noopener">MIT</a>). I previously studied Engineering Science (Robotics Option) at the University of Toronto.
